Learn basic English grammar rules and important vocabulary words!

Grammar Rules:

  • Use "a" before words that start with consonant sounds (e.g., a cat).
  • Use "an" before words that start with vowel sounds (e.g., an apple).
  • Always capitalize the first letter of a sentence.
  • Use "is" for singular and "are" for plural subjects.

Vocabulary:

Apple - Olma

Car - Mashina

Book - Kitob

House - Uy
